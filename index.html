Good to know

Questions, answered

Is the song really made just for us? +

Yes. Every song is composed around the names, details, and memories you share, so no two Heart Notes are ever alike.

How long does it take to arrive? +

Most songs are ready within 24 hours of your order. If you're on a tight timeline for a special date, contact our team and we'll do our best to help.

Can I request changes to my song? +

To keep our prices affordable for everyone, revising a song that's already been produced carries a $34 fee. That's why we recommend choosing a package with more than one version to pick from, and sharing as much detail as you can in your request, so your song feels right the very first time.

What exactly do I receive? +

A beautifully produced song on your own private gift page, with the link sent straight to your email. You can play it on any device, keep it forever, and share it with family and friends however you like.

Is it easy to order? +

Wonderfully easy. If you can write a few sentences about your loved one, you can create a Heart Note. Our team is always here if you'd like a hand.
