Good to know

Questions, answered

Is the song really made just for us? +

Yes. Every song is composed around the names, details, and memories you share, so no two Heart Notes are ever alike.

How long does it take to arrive? +

Most songs are ready within 24 hours of your order. If you're on a tight timeline for a special date, give us a call and we'll do our best to help.

What exactly do I receive? +

A beautifully produced song on your own private gift page, with the link sent straight to your email. You can play it on any phone or computer, download it, and forward it to family and friends however you like.

Do I need to be good with technology? +

Not at all. If you can fill out a short form, you can create a Heart Note. And our team is always a phone call away if you'd like a hand.
