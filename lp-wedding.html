Good to know

Questions, answered

Do we hear it before the wedding? +

Yes, and well before. Your song arrives within 24 hours of ordering, so there's no waiting and no surprises on the day. On the Deluxe and Wedding packages you also hear short clips of two or three versions and choose the one you love.

What file do we give our DJ or band? +

A studio-quality WAV, plus an MP3 for your phone. WAV is what venues and DJs ask for, and it's included on every package except Single. You can forward the file or the private link straight to them.

How much do we need to tell you? +

About five minutes' worth. Your names, where you met, the proposal, and one or two things only the two of you would find funny. The small details are what make people cry.

Can we choose the style of music? +

Of course. Acoustic, country, soul, a big string ballad, upbeat pop for the reception. You tell us the mood of the moment and we write to it.

Can we get more than one song? +

Yes. The Wedding Package is three full songs for $199, and any song after that is $49. Most couples pick the first dance, the father-daughter dance, and one for walking the aisle.

What if it isn't quite right? +

Tell us and we'll do everything we can to make it right. It's also why we suggest a package with two or three versions for something as important as a wedding, so you can choose the one that feels most like you.

Is our payment safe? +

Completely. Payments are handled by Stripe, the same secure checkout used by many of the world's largest stores. We never see or store your card details.
