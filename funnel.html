Good to know

Questions, answered

Is the song really made just for us? +

Yes. Every song is built around the names, details, and memories you share, so no two Heart Notes are ever alike.

How much do I have to write? +

About five minutes' worth. Six short questions, and a few sentences about your loved one is plenty. The more little details you give us, the more it will sound like them.

When will it arrive? +

Within 24 hours of your order. If you're on a tight timeline for a special date, contact our team and we'll do our best to help.

What exactly do I receive? +

A beautifully produced song on your own private gift page, with the link sent straight to your email. Play it on any device, keep it forever, and share it with family and friends however you like.

What if it isn't quite right? +

Tell us and we'll do everything we can to make it right. That's also why the Deluxe and Experience packages give you two or three versions to choose from, so you can pick the one that feels most like them.

Is it easy to order? +

Wonderfully easy. If you can write a few sentences about your loved one, you can create a Heart Note. Our team is always here if you'd like a hand.

Is my payment safe? +

Completely. Payments are handled by Stripe, the same secure checkout used by many of the world's largest stores. We never see or store your card details.
