Good to know

Questions, answered

Is it really made just for them? +

Yes. Every song is written from the story you give us and professionally produced for you, with their name and their details in the words. Nobody else has it, and nobody else ever will.

How much do I have to write? +

About five minutes' worth. Their name, who you are to them, one or two things they're known for, and a memory that always comes up. The small details are what make people cry.

When will it arrive? +

Within 24 hours of ordering, straight to your inbox. Most people order a few days out so there's nothing to worry about on the morning of the party.

Can I play it at the party or send it to family? +

Both. You get a file you can play from your phone or a speaker, and a private link you can forward to anyone in the family. It's yours to keep and share forever.

Can it mention the grandchildren by name? +

Please do include them. Names, nicknames, the town they grew up in, the dish they always make. Those are the lines that get replayed all night.

What if it isn't quite right? +

Tell us and we'll do everything we can to make it right. It's also why most people choose Deluxe: you hear two versions and keep the one that sounds most like them.

Is my payment safe? +

Completely. Payments are handled by Stripe, the same secure checkout used by many of the world's largest stores. We never see or store your card details.
