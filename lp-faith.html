Good to know

Questions, answered

Is the song really written for us? +

Yes. Nothing is taken off a shelf. Your song is written from your story — their name, the occasion, the words you'd want said over them — and then professionally produced for you. Nobody else has it.

Can you include a favourite verse or psalm? +

Of course. Write the passage into your order and tell us what it means to them, and we'll weave it through the song. Many people build the whole chorus around a line that has carried their family for years.

Can you name our church or our minister? +

Yes, and it's usually the line that gets the biggest reaction. Names of people and places, the year the doors opened, a nickname the congregation uses — put it all in the form and we'll use it.

What styles of music can you do? +

A traditional hymn with organ or piano, gospel with a full choir behind it, contemporary worship, or something soft and acoustic for a small gathering at home. You tell us the mood of the day and we write to it.

When will it arrive? +

Within 24 hours of your order, straight to your email. You can play it from your phone, download it, or forward the file to whoever is handling the music on the day.

What if it isn't quite right? +

Tell us and we'll do everything we can to make it right. It's also why most people choose Deluxe for an occasion that matters — you hear two versions and keep the one that feels right.

Is my payment safe? +

Completely. Payments are handled by Stripe, the same secure checkout used by many of the world's largest stores. We never see or store your card details.
